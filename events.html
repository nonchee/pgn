Along with hosting a variety of professional, social and philanthropy events, the Brothers of Phi Gamma Nu are also dedicated to a few annual signature events that have become tradition for our chapter. Our Brothers work hard to organize these events and each year we continue to see improvement and success.




Silver Lake Case Competition

Phi Gamma Nu is proud to work with top private equity (PE) firm Silver Lake in hosting a unique undergraduate PE case competition in the spring semester. Contestants come from some of the most prestigious universities, forming teams of up to 4 to compete for the top $1000 prize and recognition from the firm's partners.

Find out more about Silver Lake here.

IFair

Every year during the first week of the spring semester, Phi Gamma Nu hosts an Internship Fair in the Forum of Huntsman Hall. It's the largest student-run career fair on campus, connecting hundreds of student with companies like UBS, Deloitte, Macy's, BlackRock and more.

Brothers who have successfully gone through the recruitment process also offer resume reviews and tips.

For more information, please visit the IFair website.




Balls4Balls

Balls4Balls is a meatball eating competition held during Penn's annual relay for life, and is our chapter's signature philanthropy event. Brothers have a ton of fun organizing the event, and so do the participants during the contest. All proceeds from the event benefit the Lance Armstrong Foundation.

Find out more at our Balls4Balls website.
